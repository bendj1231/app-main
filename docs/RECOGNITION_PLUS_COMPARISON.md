## Priority System

Our priority system is designed to be fair and merit-based. Users can earn priority through two paths:

### Priority Paths

1. **Recognition Plus (Paid)**
   - Recognition Plus members get priority listing in operator selection pools
   - Profile is shown first to operators when they review interest pools
   - Clear badge indicates Recognition Plus status

2. **Foundation Program (Effort-Based)**
   - Free users who complete 50+ hours of mentorship in the Foundation Program also get priority
   - Recognizes demonstrated experience and commitment
   - Clear badge indicates Foundation Program completion

3. **Both - Top Priority**
   - Having both Recognition Plus AND Foundation Program completion puts you at the very top
   - Maximum visibility to operators
   - Best chance of being selected

### Pool of Interest System

When pilots express interest in a pathway:
- Profile is added to a "Pool of Interest" for that pathway
- Operators review the pool and handpick candidates based on their needs
- Priority status is clearly shown to help operators make informed decisions
- Recognition Plus and Foundation Program completions are highlighted

This creates a win-win system: either you pay for Recognition Plus, or you put in the effort through the Foundation Program (or both for maximum priority).
