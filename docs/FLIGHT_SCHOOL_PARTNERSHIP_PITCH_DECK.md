**Current Challenges for Flight Schools**

1. **Low Placement Rates**
   - Only 15-20% of flight school graduates secure airline positions within 6 months
   - Schools struggle to demonstrate ROI to prospective students
   - Limited visibility into airline hiring pipelines

2. **Disconnected Career Path**
   - Students lack clear pathway from training to employment
   - Schools have limited tools to track student career progression
   - No standardized metrics for school-to-airline success rates

3. **Competitive Pressure**
   - 2,500+ flight schools competing for students globally
   - Differentiation increasingly difficult
   - Rising operational costs require new revenue streams
