## Flight School Partnership Agreement

### PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**BETWEEN:**
Atlas Aviation Inc. ("Atlas")
AND
[Flight School Name] ("Partner")

**Effective Date:** [Date]

### 1. Partnership Overview

This agreement establishes a partnership between Atlas Aviation and Partner to provide career development and placement services to Partner's students through the Atlas Aviation platform.

### 2. Partnership Tier

**Selected Tier:** [ ] Basic [ ] Premium [ ] Strategic

**Tier Benefits:**
- Basic: Platform access, standard matching, basic analytics
- Premium: Priority matching, advanced analytics, white-labeling, dedicated support
- Strategic: Custom integrations, revenue sharing, executive sponsorship

### 3. Services Provided by Atlas

Atlas shall provide Partner with:

3.1 **Platform Access**
- Student accounts on Atlas Aviation platform
- Career development tools and resources
- Job matching with partner airlines
- Mentorship program access

3.2 **Matching Services**
- AI-powered candidate-to-job matching
- Airline partnership connections
- Application tracking and management
- Interview scheduling support

3.3 **Analytics & Reporting**
- Student progress tracking
- Placement rate analytics
- Engagement metrics
- Custom reports (Premium/Strategic tiers)

3.4 **Support Services**
- Email support (Basic)
- Dedicated account manager (Premium)
- Executive sponsor (Strategic)
- Technical support (all tiers)

### 4. Partner Responsibilities

Partner agrees to:

4.1 **Student Onboarding**
- Promote platform to students
- Provide student data for matching
- Encourage student engagement
- Collect feedback from students

4.2 **Data Provision**
- Provide accurate student information
- Update data regularly
- Maintain data quality
- Comply with data protection laws

4.3 **Brand Usage**
- Use approved Atlas Aviation branding
- Follow brand guidelines
- Obtain approval for co-branded materials
- Protect Atlas intellectual property

4.4 **Payment Obligations**
- Pay applicable partnership fees
- Maintain payment method on file
- Pay invoices within 30 days
- Notify Atlas of payment issues

### 5. Fees and Payment

5.1 **Partnership Fees**

**Basic Tier:** $0/month
**Premium Tier:** $2,500/month
**Strategic Tier:** Custom pricing

5.2 **Revenue Sharing** (Strategic Tier Only)
- 20% of student subscription fees
- 25% of placement success fees
- Custom revenue share agreements

5.3 **Payment Terms**
- Monthly invoicing
- Net 30 payment terms
- Late payment fee: 1.5% per month
- Suspension for non-payment after 60 days

### 6. Term and Termination

6.1 **Term**
- Initial term: 12 months
- Auto-renewal: 12-month increments
- Notice required for non-renewal: 30 days

6.2 **Termination for Cause**
Either party may terminate for material breach with 30-day notice.

6.3 **Termination Effects**
- Access to platform ceases
- Data export provided within 30 days
- Outstanding fees due immediately
- Confidentiality obligations survive

### 7. Data Protection and Privacy

7.1 **Data Ownership**
- Partner retains ownership of student data
- Atlas licenses data for platform operation
- Data returned upon termination

7.2 **Data Security**
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- AES-256 encryption
- Regular security audits
- Breach notification within 72 hours

7.3 **Compliance**
- GDPR compliance
- CCPA compliance
- Student consent management
- Data retention policies

### 8. Intellectual Property

8.1 **Atlas IP**
- Platform and technology
- Branding and trademarks
- Proprietary algorithms
- Documentation and materials

8.2 **Partner IP**
- Student data
- School branding
- Training materials
- Custom configurations

8.3 **License Grants**
- Non-exclusive license to use platform
- Right to use approved branding
- License terminates on agreement end

### 9. Confidentiality

9.1 **Confidential Information**
- Business information
- Technical data
- Student data
- Partnership terms

9.2 **Obligations**
- Protect confidentiality
- Use only for partnership purposes
- Return/destroy upon termination
- Survives termination

### 10. Warranties and Representations

10.1 **Atlas Warranties**
- Platform will perform as described
- Services provided with reasonable skill
- Compliance with applicable laws
- Security measures as specified

10.2 **Partner Warranties**
- Authority to enter agreement
- Student data accuracy
- Compliance with data laws
- No conflicting agreements

### 11. Limitation of Liability

11.1 **Cap on Liability**
- Direct damages limited to fees paid
- Indirect damages excluded
- Cap: 12 months of fees

11.2 **Exclusions**
- Gross negligence
- Willful misconduct
- Data breach due to Atlas fault

### 12. Indemnification

12.1 **Partner Indemnification**
- Partner indemnifies Atlas for:
  - Student data violations
  - Partner misconduct
  - Partner warranty breaches

12.2 **Atlas Indemnification**
- Atlas indemnifies Partner for:
  - IP infringement claims
  - Data breaches (Atlas fault)
  - Warranty breaches

### 13. Marketing and Publicity

13.1 **Joint Marketing**
- Co-branded materials with approval
- Press releases with mutual consent
- Case study development
- Event participation

13.2 **Use of Name**
- Atlas may use Partner name in:
  - Partner lists (with consent)
  - Case studies (with approval)
  - Marketing materials (with approval)

### 14. Dispute Resolution

14.1 **Negotiation**
- Good faith negotiation first
- Executive escalation if needed
- 30-day negotiation period

14.2 **Mediation**
- Mediation if negotiation fails
- Neutral mediator selected jointly
- Costs shared equally

14.3 **Arbitration**
- Binding arbitration if mediation fails
- AAA rules
- Venue: [State]
- Governing law: [State] law

### 15. General Provisions

15.1 **Entire Agreement**
- Supersedes all prior agreements
- Amendments in writing
- No oral modifications

15.2 **Severability**
- Invalid provisions severed
- Remaining provisions enforceable
- Replaced with valid provision

15.3 **Assignment**
- No assignment without consent
- Atlas may assign to successor
- Partner may assign with Atlas consent

15.4 **Force Majeure**
- Excused performance for events beyond control
- Notice within 10 days
- Reasonable efforts to mitigate

15.5 **Notices**
- Written notice required
- Email notice acceptable
- Addresses as specified
