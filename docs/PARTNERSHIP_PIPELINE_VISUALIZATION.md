## Pipeline Overview

The partnership pipeline tracks all potential and active partnerships across four key stakeholder types. This visualization provides transparency into partnership health, conversion rates, and revenue potential.

**Pipeline Stages:**
1. **Identification** - Target identified, outreach planned
2. **Outreach** - Initial contact made, meeting scheduled
3. **Discovery** - Needs assessment, value proposition presented
4. **Negotiation** - Terms discussed, agreement drafted
5. **Signed** - Contract executed, onboarding initiated
6. **Active** - Partner fully onboarded, generating revenue
7. **At Risk** - Partner showing signs of churn
8. **Churned** - Partnership terminated
