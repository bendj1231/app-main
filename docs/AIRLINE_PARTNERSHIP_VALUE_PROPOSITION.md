## Problem Statement for Airlines

### Current Hiring Challenges

**High Costs**
- Average cost per hire: $15,000-$25,000
- Recruitment agency fees: 20-25% of first-year salary
- Advertising and job board costs: $5,000-$10,000 per position
- Interview and assessment costs: $2,000-$5,000 per candidate

**Time-Consuming Process**
- Average time-to-hire: 90-180 days
- Resume screening: 40-80 hours per position
- Interview scheduling: 20-40 hours per position
- Background checks: 2-4 weeks per candidate

**Quality Uncertainty**
- No industry-standard pilot credential
- Difficulty validating candidate claims
- Poor fit rates: 30-40% of hires don't work out
- Limited insight into career progression

**Market Blindness**
- No visibility into pilot supply pipeline
- Limited market intelligence
- No data on training pipeline
- Reactive rather than proactive hiring
