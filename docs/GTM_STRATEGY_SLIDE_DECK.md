## Slide 22: Risk Mitigation

### GTM Risks & Mitigations

**Risk 1: Slow Pilot Acquisition**
- Mitigation: Diversified channels, partnership focus, viral mechanics
- Backup: Focus on flight school referrals (zero CAC)

**Risk 2: High CAC**
- Mitigation: Focus on organic and referral channels
- Backup: Reduce paid spend, increase content marketing

**Risk 3: Low Conversion**
- Mitigation: Improve onboarding, add value demonstrations
- Backup: Adjust pricing, offer trials

**Risk 4: Geographic Expansion Failure**
- Mitigation: Local partnerships, localized content
- Backup: Focus on successful regions, pause expansion

**Risk 5: Partnership Rejection**
- Mitigation: Strong value proposition, ROI calculator
- Backup: Focus on easier partnerships (flight schools)
