## Slide 3: Target Customer Segments

### Primary Segment: Career-Focused Pilots
- **Demographics:** 25-45 years old, 500-5,000 flight hours
- **Psychographics:** Ambitious, career-driven, willing to invest in growth
- **Pain Points:** Career uncertainty, lack of guidance, wrong type ratings
- **Value Proposition:** Clear career path, recognition score, AI coaching

### Secondary Segment: Flight Students
- **Demographics:** 18-30 years old, in training programs
- **Psychographics:** Future-focused, seeking direction
- **Pain Points:** Unclear career path, training decisions
- **Value Proposition:** Early career guidance, pathway planning

### Tertiary Segment: Experienced Pilots
- **Demographics:** 40+ years old, 5,000+ flight hours
- **Psychographics:** Seeking advancement, recognition
- **Pain Points:** Career plateau, lack of visibility
- **Value Proposition:** Recognition score, mentorship opportunities

## Slide 7: Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

**Free Tier (Lead Capture)**
- Profile creation
- Basic job listings
- Limited pathway access
- Target: 70% of registered pilots

**Premium Tier ($100/year or $50/6 months)**
- Pilot Recognition Score
- AI career coaching
- Job intelligence notifications
- Priority visibility
- Advanced analytics
- Target: 30% conversion rate

**Psychology**
- $100/year signals premium quality
- Higher price filters for serious pilots
- Free tier anchors premium price
- Flexible payment options

**Value Argument**
- Pilot training: $50K-$200K+
- Platform: $100/year = 0.05% of investment
- "Protect your career investment"

## Slide 12: Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Slower User Acquisition
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive event strategy, referral program acceleration
- **Backup:** Focus on high-value segments, increase marketing spend

### Risk 2: Lower Conversion Rates
- **Mitigation:** Improve value demonstration, free trials, A/B testing
- **Backup:** Adjust pricing, add features, improve onboarding

### Risk 3: Partnership Resistance
- **Mitigation:** Focus on win-win value, start with smaller partners
- **Backup:** Direct-to-pilot acquisition, organic growth focus

### Risk 4: Competitive Entry
- **Mitigation:** Move fast, build network effects, strengthen differentiation
- **Backup:** Double down on unique features (recognition score, AI coaching)
