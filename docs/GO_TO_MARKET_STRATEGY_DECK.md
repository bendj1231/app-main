## Slide 9: Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

### LTV Calculation

**Assumptions:**
- Average subscription duration: 4 years
- Average revenue: $85/year
- Churn rate: 12%/year

**LTV Formula:**
```
LTV = Average Revenue × Average Duration
LTV = $85 × 4 = $340
```

**With Referrals:**
- Referral value: $20 per pilot
- 2 referrals per pilot average
- Total LTV: $340 + $40 = $380

### LTV by Cohort

| Cohort | LTV (1 yr) | LTV (2 yrs) | LTV (3 yrs) | LTV (Lifetime) |
|--------|------------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| Early Adopters | $85 | $160 | $225 | $340 |
| Growth Phase | $85 | $165 | $235 | $355 |
| Scale Phase | $85 | $170 | $245 | $370 |
| **Average** | **$85** | **$165** | **$235** | **$355** |
